Open Data Platform

OpenPinas provides open, structured datasets and visualizations tracking Philippine political power, business connections, and major events. All data is available as JSON files you can verify, remix, or extend.

  • Political Dynasties: 50+ dynasties, 450+ members, 71 provinces tracked with network relationships
  • Business Connections: PBA teams, major conglomerates, media ownership, and political links
  • Startup Ecosystem: Startups, founders, investors, funding, and connections to power structures
  • Timeline Data: Major events from 2025 and 2026 with dynasty connections and impact analysis
  • Weekly Reviews: Curated news digests with weather, exchange rates, and OFW updates
  • Economic Data: PHP:USD exchange rates, airport passenger traffic, travel patterns
Sources and related projects Ateneo Policy Center PCIJ Dynasties BetterGov Dynasty Tracker Business Connections Docs

Available Datasets

All OpenPinas data is available as open JSON files. Download, verify, remix, or extend any dataset.

  • Political Dynasties: 50+ dynasties with relationships, geographic control, and business connections
  • Business Connections: PBA teams, conglomerates, media ownership, and political links
  • 2025 Timeline: Major events with dynasty connections and impact analysis
  • 2026 Timeline: Ongoing events tracking (updated weekly)
  • Elite Schools: Educational institutions and their influence networks
  • POGO Corruption Cases: Documented corruption cases related to Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators
  • Exchange Rates: Weekly PHP:USD exchange rate data
  • Airport Data: Passenger traffic patterns for major Philippine airports
