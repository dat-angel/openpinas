OpenPinas

An open dataset tracking 59 political dynasties controlling Philippine politics. Built for overseas Filipinos trying to make sense of what's happening back home.

67% of Congress seats are held by dynasty members. In 2025, the flood control scandal exposed how deep the rot goes—implicating the House Speaker, Senate President, and billions in stolen funds.

Why this exists

I'm part of the Filipino diaspora. Every family group chat becomes a political debate, but it's hard to follow who's related to whom, who switched sides, and who's suddenly in jail.

This started as a spreadsheet. Then I realized other kababayans might find it useful too. The data is sourced from Rappler, Inquirer, PCIJ, and official government records. It's not complete—Philippine politics moves fast—but it's a start.

If you spot errors or want to contribute, the data is on GitHub. PRs welcome.
