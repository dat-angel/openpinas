Background

I live abroad. Every visit home, the political landscape has shifted—new alliances, new scandals, someone's cousin is now governor. The family Viber group doesn't help.

So I started tracking it. Dynasty names, who married whom, which provinces they control, what they're accused of. Data from Rappler, Inquirer, PCIJ, Wikipedia, and way too many hours reading Senate hearing transcripts.

It's incomplete. It's probably got errors. But it's on GitHub if you want to fix something or add data.
