About This Project

This tracker documents corruption cases and POGO-related crimes in the Philippines, with a focus on transparency and accountability. Cases are tracked from filing through resolution, including political connections and impacts on Filipino communities.

Current Focus: POGO-related cases including identity fraud, human trafficking, tax evasion, and corruption. The tracker includes high-profile cases like the Alice Guo investigation.
