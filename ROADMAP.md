## üìä Priority Areas

Based on community feedback, we're prioritizing:

1. **Data completeness** - More dynasties, more events, more cases
2. **Data accuracy** - Better fact-checking and verification
3. **Usability** - Easier to use, better mobile experience
4. **Accessibility** - Works for everyone, regardless of technical skill
5. **Documentation** - Clear guides for contributors and users
