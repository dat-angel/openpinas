Business Connections

Dataset tracking business-political connections: PBA team ownership, major conglomerates, media ownership, and their links to political dynasties.

Dataset Overview

The Business Connections dataset tracks the intersection of business and political power in the Philippines, including sports organizations, major corporations, media outlets, and their connections to political dynasties.

Data Coverage

Sports Organizations

  • PBA Teams: 12 teams with ownership details and political connections
  • Olympic Organizations: Philippine Olympic Committee (POC) and Samahang Basketbol ng Pilipinas (SBP)
  • Athlete Political Connections: Notable athletes with political involvement

Major Corporations

  • Conglomerates: 9 major business groups (San Miguel, Ayala, SM, MVP, Villar, JG Summit, LT Group, Aboitiz, GT Capital)
  • Media Ownership: 7 major media outlets (ABS-CBN, GMA, TV5, Philippine Star, Manila Bulletin, Philippine Daily Inquirer, Rappler)
  • Government Contracts: Structure for tracking infrastructure and public works contracts
  • Campaign Financing: Structure for tracking political campaign contributions

Integration with OpenPinas

Business connections are integrated with OpenPinas dynasty data through the business_connections field in the dynasty JSON file.

Linked Dynasties

  • VILLAR_FAMILY: Villar Group (real estate, retail, agriculture), AMBS media ownership
  • BELMONTE_QUEZON_CITY: Philippine Star ownership
  • LOPEZ_ILOILO: ABS-CBN ownership (historical)
  • COJUANGCO_TARLAC: San Miguel Corporation connections
  • AQUINO_FAMILY: Historical business connections
