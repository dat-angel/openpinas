2025

  • December 2025

    Marcos-Duterte Rift "Beyond Repair"

    The political alliance between the Marcos and Duterte families that won them the 2022 election deteriorated throughout 2025, with impeachment proceedings, public accusations, and the ICC arrest of former President Duterte.

    Source: Inquirer

  • July 25, 2025

    Supreme Court Voids Sara Duterte Impeachment

    The Supreme Court ruled 13-0 that the impeachment complaint against Vice President Sara Duterte was unconstitutional, citing the "one-year bar rule." No new complaints can be filed until February 6, 2026.

    Source: Rappler, Al Jazeera

  • March 28, 2025

    Rodrigo Duterte Surrendered to ICC

    Former President Rodrigo Duterte was arrested at NAIA and surrendered to the International Criminal Court, becoming the first Asian former head of state to be arrested by the ICC. He faces charges of crimes against humanity related to the drug war.

    Source: Wikipedia

  • March 11, 2025

    Rodrigo Duterte Arrested at NAIA

    Former President Rodrigo Duterte was arrested at Ninoy Aquino International Airport in relation to ICC cases. His son, Davao City Mayor Sebastian Duterte, cursed at President Marcos for "allowing his father to be jailed."

    Source: PhilStar
