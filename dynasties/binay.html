Historical Context

  • 2010-2016

    Jejomar Binay as Vice President

    Jejomar Binay served as Vice President of the Philippines under President Noynoy Aquino from 2010-2016. He ran for president in 2016 but finished fourth amid corruption allegations.

    Historical record

  • 1987-2010

    Makati Mayorship

    Jejomar Binay served as Mayor of Makati for a total of 21 years (1986-1998, 2001-2010), transforming it into the financial capital of the Philippines and establishing the family's political base.

    Historical record

  • 2013-2022

    Nancy Binay in the Senate

    Nancy Binay served in the Senate from 2013-2022, continuing the family's national political presence while her father was Vice President.
