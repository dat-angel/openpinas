Historical Context

  • 2001-2010

    Gloria Macapagal Arroyo Presidency

    Gloria Macapagal Arroyo served as president from 2001-2010, taking office after the ouster of Joseph Estrada. Her administration was marked by economic growth but also allegations of electoral fraud and corruption.

    Historical record

  • 2018-2019

    House Speaker

    Gloria Macapagal Arroyo served as Speaker of the House of Representatives from 2018-2019 during the Duterte administration, marking her return to a powerful position after years of legal battles.

    Historical record

  • 1961-1965

    Diosdado Macapagal Presidency

    Gloria's father, Diosdado Macapagal, served as president from 1961-1965. He is credited with moving Philippine Independence Day from July 4 to June 12 and implementing economic liberalization policies.

    Historical record

  • Family Legacy

    Two-Generation Presidential Dynasty

    The Macapagal-Arroyo family is one of only two families in Philippine history to have produced two presidents (father and daughter), alongside the Aquino family (mother Cory and son Noynoy).
