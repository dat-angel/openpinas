This archive tracks news coverage of major Philippine political dynasties. Each dynasty page aggregates all related news mentions from weekly reviews, the timeline, and external sources.

By 2025, approximately 80% of provincial governors belong to "fat dynasties" (multiple family members in power simultaneously), up from 57% in 2004. This project aims to make these connections transparent and accessible.
